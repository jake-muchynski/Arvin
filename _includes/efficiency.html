  • The Arvin Advantage

  • Case Study:Pollard Windows Saves $68,000 in Two Years

  • Pollard's Problem

  • The 53 stand alone unit heaters were controlled by individual thermostats. The plant manager endured complaints from shop personnel of temperature fluctuations, caused by adjusting thermostats resulting in overheating & higher energy costs.

  • Our Solution

  • The Arvin Air System’s team linked 53 unit heaters in 4 separate buildings and allocated them to specific zones. All of the unit heaters are now controlled by a keypad in the plant manager’s office. This system enables the plant manager to set and monitor temperatures in all areas of the facility and configure setback times.

  • The Result

  • Employees comfort is enhanced and plant temperature fluctuations are minimized. Also, in two years Pollard Windows saved over $68,000 in natural gas expenditures.
